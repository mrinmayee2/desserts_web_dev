How to make brownie ice cream?

Step 1- Whip the cream. In a large mixing bowl, add the heavy cream and beat until soft peaks form. Step 2- Combine ingredients. To the bowl, add the condensed milk and cocoa powder and beat until thick and fluffy. Fold through the brownies. Step 3- Freeze. Transfer the ice cream mixture to a loaf pan, cover, and freeze for at least an hour.
